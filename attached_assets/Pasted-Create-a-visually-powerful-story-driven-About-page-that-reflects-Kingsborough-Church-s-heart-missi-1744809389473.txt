Create a visually powerful, story-driven About page that reflects Kingsborough Church’s heart, mission, and spiritual foundation — using modern layout, elegant typography, and scroll-triggered motion.

🎨 Color Palette:
Gold (#D4AF37): For key highlights and accents.

Lilac (#C8A2C8): For soft section backgrounds and overlays.

White (#FFFFFF): Dominant color for clarity and balance.

Optional Black or Deep Charcoal for text-heavy sections to create contrast.

📐 Page Structure (Mirroring JoinCCI Flow):
1. Intro Section (Full-Screen Hero)
Large, bold mission statement or church motto (e.g., “We are a light in the city”)

Subtext with 1-2 lines about Kingsborough’s identity

Background: Subtle gold-tinted video loop or parallax image of worship/service

Animated scroll-down indicator

2. “Who We Are” Split Layout
Left: Large heading + short 3–4 sentence description

Right: Full-height background image or looping muted video

Smooth scroll reveal effects on text

Optional: Add logo watermark in background using low opacity

3. Our Story (Timeline Style Section)
Horizontal or vertical timeline (GSAP-style or progressive scroll)

Each milestone animates into view

Use lilac blocks with gold text accents

Include 2–4 key moments in the church’s journey

4. Our Vision & Mission
Full-width layout

Bold typography: “Our Vision” and “Our Mission” side-by-side or stacked

Add parallax scrolling text or gold hover underline effect

5. Core Values Grid / Cards
Card-based section with 3 to 6 values (e.g., Love, Service, Growth, Community)

Hover effects: color glow or subtle scale-up

Optionally animate in each card as user scrolls

6. Meet the Team
Stylish image cards for Pastors and Ministry Leads

On hover: name + role fade in, or flip card to show a short bio

Consistent gold/lilac border or shadow for cohesion

7. Join Our Story (Call to Action)
Full-screen call-to-action

“Join the Vision. Walk the Journey. Be the Light.” (custom message)

Large gold button: “Plan a Visit” / “Watch a Service” / “Meet the Team”

✨ Visual/UX Touches:
Subtle scroll-triggered fades and transitions

Parallax backgrounds on 2-3 sections

Use of decorative golden lines/dividers for section transitions

Fixed navigation that updates as user scrolls

Elegant serif headings paired with readable sans-serif body font

Animated text overlays or SVG icons to complement value sections

🧑‍💻 Technical Considerations:
Mobile-optimized (stacked layout, collapsible team cards)

Lazy-loaded images for performance

Scroll animation via Framer Motion, GSAP, or locomotive-scroll

CMS-friendly structure for updating team members or story milestones
