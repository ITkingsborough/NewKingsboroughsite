Rebuild the entire website for Kingsborough Church with a modern, visually immersive design that reflects spiritual depth, warmth, and community. The new site should reflect the aesthetic and interactive experience of sites like JoinCCI.org and ElevationChurch.org, incorporating advanced visual effects, parallax scrolling, and a refined, elegant feel.

🎨 Color Scheme:
Gold (#D4AF37 or similar): Symbolizes royalty, divine presence, and excellence. Used for highlights, buttons, and accent elements.

Lilac (#C8A2C8 or similar): Represents spiritual awakening and peace. Can be used for backgrounds, section dividers, and soft overlays.

White (#FFFFFF): For clarity, cleanliness, and balance — dominant background color for readability and simplicity.

✨ Design Goals:
Full-screen hero section with animated background or parallax video loop (e.g., aerial city, people worshipping, or symbolic movement).

Smooth parallax transitions between major sections.

Animated elements such as text reveals, fade-ins, scroll-triggered effects, and dynamic backgrounds.

Floating navigation bar with smooth scroll and highlight effects.

Bold typography, modern layout, and strong visual hierarchy.

Mobile-first design with responsive scaling across all device types.
